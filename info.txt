Expense tracker logic 

** balance is constantly updating by calculating total income and expense and are stored to the local-storage.

** history is constantly updated and filtered by the input whether of not the amount 
   has a minus(-) sign and is stored to the local storage.

** income and expense section is constantly updated and filtered by the input values
   whether or not they have the minus(-) sign.

** and the add button stimulates all of the operations except for the delete.

** Delete button to delete from the history and removes all of its calcuations.
